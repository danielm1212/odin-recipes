lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's hearty, satisfying, and even better the next day.

This recipe serves 6 people and takes about 90 minutes from start to finish.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 jar tomato sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef with garlic, then add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and top with parmesan.
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil for the last 15.
  7. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
