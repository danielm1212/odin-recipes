Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A hearty Italian pasta dish with a slow-cooked meat sauce. Simple ingredients, big flavour.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Olive oil, salt, pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Saut√© onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  3. Add ground beef and brown it.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
